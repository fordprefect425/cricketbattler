## ğŸ® How to Play
1.  **Draft/Deploy:** You start with 5 Cards and 1 Energy.
2.  **Play Cards:** Click a card (Gold Selection) then Click a Lane.
3.  **Lanes:**
    *   **Green Top:** Bowlers get buffs.
    *   **Dust Bowl:** Spinners dominate.
    *   **Flat Track:** Batters paradise.
4.  **End Turn:** Click the Red Button to resolve combat.
5.  **Win Condition:** Win 2 out of 3 lanes by having a higher score after 6 Turns.
